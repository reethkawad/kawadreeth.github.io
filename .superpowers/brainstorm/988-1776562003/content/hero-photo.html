How should your photo appear on the first page?

The hero is currently text-only. Here are three options for placing your photo there.

A

Side-by-side split

Photo on the right, name + tagline on the left. Classic professional layout — common in portfolios. Photo is roughly half the screen width.

B

Centered with photo above text

Circular or square photo centered at top of hero, name + tagline below. Personal, warm feel. The industry zone portals stay below.

C

Keep About section, add small avatar to hero

Small circular avatar (64–80px) next to your name in the hero — subtle presence. Full photo remains in the About section below. Minimal change.

Note: your full-size photo currently lives in the About Me section. Options A and B would move it to the hero. Option C keeps it in both places (small avatar in hero, full photo in About).
