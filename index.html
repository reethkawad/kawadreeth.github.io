8 DoF Robotic Hand

The image is the CAD progress so far. Making a robotic hand for the Dynamic and Robotic Controls Lab (DRCL). It has two fingers and a thumb. The DIP's are passive linked to PIP joints, which are actuated using N20 high geared motors. The MCP joint has a spring damper and a 20kgcm servo motor for high torque and impact mitigation. The hand will be attached to a bipedal, Hector of DRCL.

My Projects

Blowdown Wind Tunnel – Flow Straightener Validation

The primary objective was to validate effectiveness of honeycomb in achieving laminar flow conditions. Designed and built a 1.5-meter-long blowdown wind tunnel with a 100×100 mm² test section, primarily using plywood and acrylic. A custom 3D-printed PLA honeycomb was integrated as a flow straightener. Through velocity profile analysis, the system demonstrated flow stabilization with Reynolds numbers up to 2000—successfully confirming the honeycomb's role in minimizing turbulence.
